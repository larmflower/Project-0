HUNGRY ZOMBIES ARE ON YOUR TRAIL


Join Columbus, Talahasse, Lil Rock and Witchita as they
navigate the zombie apocalypse. As you move along the board you will participate
in movie themed trivia questions! Answer correctly, and move forward through the
game. Should you answer incorrectly, zombies will dispose of one of your
three lives. Your objective is to reach the end of the board with enough lives entact
to reach Pacific Playland!
